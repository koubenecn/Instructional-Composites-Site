Welcome to Instructional Composites.

We are a higher education consulting company founded on five principles: that consulting (and its outcomes) must be

  • collaborative,
  • explainable,
  • actionable,
  • iterative, and
  • ethical.

Instructional Composites seeks to follow these principles on all consulting projects. Our work largely falls into two general categories: data-driven market research and learner-centered instructional design. Portfolio is available upon request.
